ENKOLILI COMMUNITY BASED ORGANIZATION 

WHO WE ARE  

Enkolili Community Based Organization is a local Community Based Organization (CBO) in Kenya. It is located in Maili Tisa, 130 kilometers from Nairobi along Namanga road. It was registered in 2022 under section 10 of the NGO Coordination ACT

The primary purpose of Enkolili Community Based Organization is to empower women and children in the Maasai pastoral community to identify and achieve their own development initiatives and promote decision making. 

The organizationâ€™s development approach is based on participation, women empowerment, local capacity building/training, gender equality, advocacy, sustainability and accountability. The organization considers the development of strong linkages with the community as a priority in the attainment of its Programmatic and organizational goals.

 The organization has extensively been involved in child protection and women empowerment in Kajiado County. The interventions anchor around child rights advocacy and women empowerment.

VISION AND MISION

Enkolili is committed to transform the lives of the most vulnerable children and their families through providing requisite support that enhances their ability to reach their full potential. Education and training eradicates illiteracy and endeavours to build a greater future by allowing children to understand their shortcomings and opportunities at hand and grant them a chance to build a better future for generations to come.

OBJECTIVES AND GOALS

1.To protect girls from Female Genital Mutilation/Cutting (FFM/C) and early marriages. 

2.To promote the rights of access to formal education.

3.To support access to justice for FGM victims.
